# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## Introduction

Montana Fishing Reports ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What**: GPS location (with your permission)
- **Why**: Show nearby rivers and access points on the map
- **Storage**: Used in real-time, not stored permanently
- **Control**: You can disable location services in device settings

### Push Notification Tokens
- **What**: Device token for push notifications
- **Why**: Send river condition alerts and fishing updates
- **Storage**: Stored securely on our servers
- **Control**: You can unsubscribe from notifications anytime

### Fishing Log Data
- **What**: Your catch entries (species, size, fly used, notes)
- **Why**: Personal fishing journal and statistics
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device
- **Control**: You can delete entries anytime

### Analytics Data
- **What**: Anonymous usage statistics (pages viewed, features used)
- **Why**: Improve app performance and user experience
- **Storage**: Aggregated and anonymized
- **Control**: No opt-out required (no personal data)

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Name, email address, or contact information
- Photos (unless you choose to add them to your fishing log)
- Financial information
- Social media profiles
- Precise location history

## How We Use Your Information

1. **To Provide Services**: Show river conditions, access points, and fishing reports
2. **To Send Notifications**: Alert you to favorable fishing conditions (if subscribed)
3. **To Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and add features
4. **To Store Your Data**: Save your fishing log entries on your device

## Data Security

- All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
- Server infrastructure secured with industry-standard practices
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- No selling or sharing of personal data with third parties
