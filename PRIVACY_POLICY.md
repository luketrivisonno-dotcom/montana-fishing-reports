# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## Introduction

Montana Fishing Reports ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data

**Precise Location (GPS)**
- **What**: Exact GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) with high accuracy
- **When**: When you "Tag Current Location" for a fishing log entry
- **Why**: To record where you caught a fish on the map
- **Storage**: Stored locally in your fishing log on your device
- **Control**: You can deny location permission or manually clear location data

**Coarse Location**
- **What**: Approximate location for map centering
- **When**: When you tap the "My Location" button on the map
- **Why**: To center the map on your general area
- **Storage**: Used in real-time, not stored permanently
- **Control**: Can be disabled in device settings

### 2. Photos and Camera

- **What**: Photos you take with your camera or select from your photo library
- **When**: Adding photos to your fishing log entries
- **Why**: To document your catches
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only, never uploaded to our servers
- **Permissions**: Camera access and Photo Library access (requested when needed)
- **Control**: You can deny permissions and photos are never shared

### 3. User Identifiers

**RevenueCat Anonymous ID**
- **What**: Anonymous unique identifier generated by RevenueCat
- **Why**: To manage your premium subscription and restore purchases across devices
- **Storage**: Stored by RevenueCat on their servers
- **Note**: This is not linked to your personal identity

**Push Notification Token**
- **What**: Device token provided by Apple/Google for push notifications
- **Why**: To send you river flow alerts and fishing condition updates (if subscribed)
- **Storage**: Stored on our servers with your subscription preferences
- **Control**: You can disable notifications in device settings

**API Key**
- **What**: Unique key generated by our backend for premium users
- **Why**: To authenticate premium features (river alerts, hatch notifications)
- **Storage**: Stored locally on device and linked to your RevenueCat ID on our servers

### 4. Purchase Data

- **What**: Subscription status, purchase history, expiration dates, product identifiers
- **Why**: To manage your premium subscription, handle renewals, and restore purchases
- **Storage**: Primary storage by RevenueCat; cached locally on your device
- **Third-party**: RevenueCat processes this data according to their privacy policy

### 5. User Content

**Fishing Log Data**
- **What**: Species caught, size, fly/lure used, notes, date, location coordinates, photos
- **Why**: Personal fishing journal and catch statistics
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Note**: This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it

**Personal Map Pins**
- **What**: Custom pins you create on the map (fishing spots, access points, hazards, notes)
- **Why**: To mark personal locations on the river map
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only

**Favorites**
- **What**: Your favorite rivers and access points
- **Why**: Quick access to frequently viewed locations
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only

**Notification Preferences**
- **What**: Which rivers you've subscribed to for flow alerts and hatch notifications
- **Why**: To send you relevant fishing condition updates
- **Storage**: Stored on our servers linked to your push token

### 6. Advertising Data

**Google Mobile Ads**
- **What**: Device ID, advertising ID (IDFA/AAID), ad interaction data
- **Why**: To display personalized or non-personalized ads to free users
- **Third-party**: Google Mobile Ads SDK collects this data per Google's privacy policy
- **Control**: Premium users see no ads. Free users can limit ad tracking in device settings.

**Ad Frequency Tracking**
- **What**: Count of how many times you've viewed certain screens (stored locally)
- **Why**: To control how often interstitial ads are shown
- **Storage**: Local only (AsyncStorage)

### 7. Analytics and Diagnostics

**Expo Analytics**
- **What**: Anonymous app usage statistics (app launches, screen views, feature usage)
- **Why**: To improve app performance and user experience
- **Storage**: Aggregated and anonymized by Expo

**Crash Reporting**
- **What**: Crash logs, error messages, device information (OS version, device model)
- **Why**: To identify and fix bugs
- **Storage**: Collected by Expo's crash reporting service
- **Note**: No personal data included in crash reports

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Name, email address, or contact information (except API key email for premium sync)
- Phone number or physical address
- Contacts from your phone
- Browsing or search history
- Financial information (credit cards, bank accounts) - all handled by Apple/Google
- Precise location history (only stored when you explicitly tag a catch)
- Social media profiles

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Fishing log, photos, personal pins, and favorites are stored **only on your device**
- **Server Storage**: Push tokens and notification preferences stored on our secure servers
- **Third-Party Storage**: RevenueCat stores purchase data; Expo stores analytics
- **Encryption**: All network communications use HTTPS encryption
- **No Sale**: We do not sell your personal data to third parties
