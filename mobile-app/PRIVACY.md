# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026

## Montana Fishing Reports

Thank you for using Montana Fishing Reports. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

---

### Information We Collect

#### **Account & Subscription Information**
- **Premium Subscription:** When you purchase a Premium subscription, we collect transaction information through RevenueCat (our payment processor). We do not store your payment card details.
- **Device Identifier:** We collect a unique device identifier to manage free usage limits and synchronize your Premium status across app reinstalls.

#### **Location Data**
- **Precise Location:** With your permission, we collect your precise location to:
  - Show your position on the map
  - Display nearby fishing rivers and access points
  - Calculate distances to river access points
- You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

#### **User-Generated Content**
- **Fishing Log:** If you use the Premium Fishing Log feature, your catch data (including photos, location, fish type, size, and notes) is stored locally on your device unless you choose to share it.
- **Favorites:** Your favorited rivers are stored locally and synced with your device identifier.

#### **Social Media Sharing**
- **Share Feature:** When you use our Brag Card or social sharing features, you control what content is shared. We do not post to social media on your behalf.
- **Third-Party Sharing:** Sharing to social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, etc.) is handled by those platforms' respective apps and subject to their privacy policies.

#### **Push Notifications**
- **Notification Preferences:** If you enable notifications (Premium feature), we collect:
  - Your push notification token
  - Subscribed rivers for alerts
  - Alert preferences (hatch alerts, flow alerts, daily briefing)
- You can manage or disable notifications anytime in Settings.

#### **Analytics & Diagnostics**
- **Usage Analytics:** We collect anonymous data about:
  - Which rivers and features are viewed
  - App performance and load times
  - Feature usage patterns
- **Crash Logs:** We collect crash reports and error logs to fix bugs and improve stability.
- **Advertising:** Free users see ads served by Google Mobile Ads. See "Third-Party Services" below.

---

### How We Use Your Information

- **Provide Core Features:** Display river data, flow conditions, fishing reports, and maps
- **Premium Services:** Manage subscriptions, enable Premium features (HatchCast, Fishing Log, Leaderboard, Notifications)
- **Personalization:** Save your favorite rivers and fishing log entries
- **Notifications:** Send alerts for new reports, hatch activity, and flow changes (Premium only)
- **Social Sharing:** Enable sharing of fishing achievements via Brag Cards
- **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and performance
- **Support:** Respond to customer service requests

---

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **RevenueCat** | Subscription management | Purchase history, device ID |
| **Google Mobile Ads** | Advertising (free users) | Device info, ad interactions |
| **Google Analytics** | Usage analytics | Anonymous usage data |
| **USGS/NOAA APIs** | River flow and weather data | Location (for nearby data) |
| **Expo** | App development platform | Crash logs, performance data |

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We only share the minimum data necessary for their functionality.

---

### Data Storage & Security

- **Local Storage:** Most data (fishing logs, favorites, cached river data) is stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Storage:** Subscription status and notification preferences are stored securely in the cloud
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
- **Data Retention:** We retain usage analytics for 12 months, then anonymize or delete

---

### Your Rights & Choices

#### **Location Access**
- Disable anytime in device Settings → Montana Fishing Reports → Location

#### **Notifications**
- Manage in-app: Settings → Notifications
- Or in device Settings → Notifications → Montana Fishing Reports

#### **Data Export**
- Premium users can export fishing log data (feature coming soon)

#### **Data Deletion**
To request deletion of your account data:
- **Email:** Dhaul12@protonmail.com
- Include your device ID (found in Settings → About)
- We process requests within 30 days

#### **Advertising Opt-Out**
- Free users can limit ad tracking in device Settings → Privacy → Tracking
- Upgrade to Premium to remove all ads
