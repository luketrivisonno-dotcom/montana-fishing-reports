Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

Montana Fishing Reports ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • What: GPS location (with your permission)
  • Why: Show nearby rivers and access points on the map
  • Storage: Used in real-time, not stored permanently
  • Control: You can disable location services in device settings

Push Notification Tokens

  • What: Device token for push notifications
  • Why: Send river condition alerts and fishing updates
  • Storage: Stored securely on our servers
  • Control: You can unsubscribe from notifications anytime

Fishing Log Data

  • What: Your catch entries (species, size, fly used, notes)
  • Why: Personal fishing journal and statistics
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device
  • Control: You can delete entries anytime

Analytics Data

  • What: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Why: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Storage: Aggregated and anonymized

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Photos (unless you add them to your fishing log)
  • Financial information
  • Social media profiles
  • Precise location history

Advertising

The free version of our app displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. This involves:

  • Device Advertising Identifier (IDFA): Used to personalize advertisements
  • Coarse Location: Used to show relevant local advertisements
  • Usage Data: Used to measure ad performance

Your Control: You can disable personalized ads at any time:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience with no advertising data collection.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • USGS Water Data: Provides river flow and temperature data
  • Weather Services: Provides local weather conditions
  • Expo: Provides app hosting and analytics
  • Google Maps: Provides map display services
  • Google Mobile Ads: Displays advertisements (free users only)
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscriptions and purchases

Data Security

All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption. We use industry-standard security practices and never sell your personal data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, delete, or opt-out of data collection. Contact us to exercise these rights.
