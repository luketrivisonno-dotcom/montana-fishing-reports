Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Montana Fishing Reports collects the following data to provide our services:

Location Data

  • What: Coarse location (approximate, not precise GPS)
  • Why: Show nearby rivers and access points on the map
  • Storage: Used in real-time, not stored permanently on our servers
  • Control: You can disable location services in device settings

User Account Data

  • User ID: Anonymous identifier generated by RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Device ID: Device identifier used to manage purchases and enable restore functionality
  • Purchase History: Subscription status and purchase records
  • Why: Manage premium subscriptions, restore purchases across devices, prevent fraud

Photos and Videos

  • What: Photos you add to your personal fishing log
  • Why: Document your catches and fishing experiences
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device, not uploaded to our servers
  • Control: You can delete photos anytime within the app

Usage Data

  • What: Anonymous app usage statistics (app launches, feature usage, interactions)
  • Why: Improve app performance and user experience
  • Storage: Aggregated and anonymized

Diagnostics

  • What: Crash logs and performance data (launch time, hang rates)
  • Why: Debug issues and improve app stability
  • Storage: Anonymous technical data only

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Phone number or physical address
  • Precise GPS location (we only use coarse/approximate location)
  • Contacts from your phone
  • Browsing or search history
  • Financial information (credit cards, bank accounts) - handled by Apple/RevenueCat

How We Use Your Data

  • Display river flows, weather, and nearby access points
  • Manage premium subscriptions and purchases (via RevenueCat)
  • Maintain your personal fishing log locally on your device
  • Send push notifications for river alerts (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • USGS Water Data: Provides river flow and temperature data
  • Weather Services: Provides local weather conditions
  • Expo: App platform, analytics, and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management, purchase tracking, and fraud prevention

Data Storage and Security

  • Fishing log entries and photos are stored locally on your device only
  • Subscription data is securely managed by RevenueCat
  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your fishing log entries and photos (done locally in the app)
  • Opt-out of location services via device settings
  • Cancel subscriptions through App Store settings
