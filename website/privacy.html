Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

Montana Fishing Reports ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data

Precise Location (GPS)

  • What: Exact GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) with high accuracy
  • When: When you "Tag Current Location" for a fishing log entry
  • Why: To record where you caught a fish on the map
  • Storage: Stored locally in your fishing log on your device
  • Control: You can deny location permission or manually clear location data

Coarse Location

  • What: Approximate location for map centering
  • When: When you tap the "My Location" button on the map
  • Why: To center the map on your general area
  • Storage: Used in real-time, not stored permanently

2. Photos and Camera

  • What: Photos you take with your camera or select from your photo library
  • When: Adding photos to your fishing log entries
  • Why: To document your catches
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only, never uploaded to our servers
  • Permissions: Camera access and Photo Library access (requested when needed)

3. User Identifiers

RevenueCat Anonymous ID

  • Anonymous unique identifier generated by RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Used to restore purchases across devices
  • Not linked to your personal identity

Push Notification Token

  • Device token provided by Apple/Google for push notifications
  • Used to send river flow alerts and fishing condition updates
  • Stored on our servers with your subscription preferences

API Key

  • Unique key generated by our backend for premium users
  • Used to authenticate premium features (river alerts, hatch notifications)

4. Purchase Data

  • Subscription status, purchase history, expiration dates, product identifiers
  • Managed by RevenueCat for handling renewals and purchase restoration
  • Cached locally on your device

5. User Content

Fishing Log Data

  • Species caught, size, fly/lure used, notes, date, location coordinates, photos
  • Stored locally on your device only
  • This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it

Personal Map Pins

  • Custom pins you create (fishing spots, access points, hazards, notes)
  • Stored locally on your device only

Favorites & Notification Preferences

  • Your favorite rivers and access points (local storage)
  • River subscriptions for alerts (stored on our servers)

6. Advertising Data (Free Users Only)

Google Mobile Ads

  • Device ID, advertising ID (IDFA/AAID), ad interaction data
  • Used to display ads to free users
  • Premium users see no ads

7. Analytics and Diagnostics

  • Expo Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics
  • Crash Reporting: Crash logs, error messages, device info (OS version, model)
  • No personal data included in analytics or crash reports

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Phone number or physical address
  • Contacts from your phone
  • Browsing or search history
  • Financial information (handled by Apple/Google)
  • Precise location history (only stored when you explicitly tag a catch)
  • Social media profiles

Third-Party Services

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Fishing log, photos, personal pins, and favorites are stored only on your device
  • Server Storage: Push tokens and notification preferences stored on our secure servers
  • Third-Party: RevenueCat stores purchase data; Expo stores analytics
  • Encryption: All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No Sale: We do not sell your personal data to third parties

Push Notifications River flow alerts and hatch notifications Yes

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request what data we have about you
  • Delete: Clear your fishing log, photos, and favorites (done locally in the app)
  • Export: Your fishing log data is accessible in the app
  • Opt-out: Disable location services, notifications, or ad tracking via device settings
  • Cancel Subscriptions: Manage subscriptions through App Store or Play Store settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy
