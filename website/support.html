Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the river flow data?

Our flow data comes directly from USGS (U.S. Geological Survey) gauge stations and is updated in real-time. The data is the same information used by government agencies and fishing guides.

Do I need an internet connection?

The app requires internet to fetch real-time data. However, once you've viewed a river, the data is cached and available offline for a short period.

How do I enable push notifications?

Tap the bell icon on any river page to subscribe to alerts for that river. You'll receive notifications when conditions change or new reports are available.

Is my fishing log data backed up?

Currently, fishing log data is stored locally on your device. We're working on cloud backup functionality for a future update.

Can you add [specific river]?

We prioritize rivers with USGS gauge stations for accurate data. If there's a specific river you'd like added, please email us with your request.

The app is crashing / not working

Try these steps:
1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Check your internet connection
3. Update to the latest app version
4. Restart your device
If problems persist, please email us with details about your device and iOS version.

Data Sources

Our app aggregates data from:

  • USGS Water Services (flow and temperature)
  • National Weather Service (weather data)
  • Montana Fish, Wildlife & Parks (access sites)
  • Local fly shops (fishing reports)
