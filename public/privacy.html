Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Montana Fishing Reports collects minimal data to provide river conditions and fishing reports:

  • Device information (for app functionality)
  • Location data (optional, for local weather and nearby rivers)
  • Usage analytics (to improve the app)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Display river flows and weather
  • Send push notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance

Advertising

The free version of our app displays advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. This involves:

  • Device Advertising Identifier (IDFA): Used to personalize advertisements
  • Coarse Location: Used to show relevant local advertisements
  • Usage Data: Used to measure ad performance

Your Control: You can disable personalized ads at any time in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience with no advertising data collection.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • USGS Water Services (flow data)
  • Open-Meteo (weather data)
  • Expo (app platform)
  • Google Mobile Ads (advertising - free users only)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

Data Storage

We do not store personal information on our servers. Your fishing log entries are stored locally on your device.
