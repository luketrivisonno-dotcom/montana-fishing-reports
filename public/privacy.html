Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

Montana Fishing Reports collects minimal data to provide river conditions and fishing reports:

  • Device information (for app functionality)
  • Location data (optional, for local weather and nearby rivers)
  • Usage analytics (to improve the app)

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Display river flows and weather
  • Send push notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance

Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • USGS Water Services (flow data)
  • Open-Meteo (weather data)
  • Expo (app platform)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

Data Storage

We do not store personal information on our servers. Your fishing log entries are stored locally on your device.
